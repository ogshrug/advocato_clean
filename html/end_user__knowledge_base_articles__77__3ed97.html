Health Practitioner

MA 927 - Digest

Health Practitioner February 12, 2026
Main uses:
  • To enhance digestion (indigestion, flatulence, etc)

Ingredients:
Each tablet contains: cane sugar 204.5 mg, Carum carvi (black caraway) 64.2 mg,
Punica granatum (pomegranate) 50.5 mg, Cuminum cyminum (cumin) 45.9 mg,
Piper longum (pippali) 13.8 mg, Zingiber officinale (ginger) 13.8 mg, Piper
nigrum (black pepper) 13.8 mg, Elettaria cardamomum (cardamom) 13.8 mg, rock
salt 13.8 mg, Cinnamomum zeylanicum (Ceylon cinnamon) 13.8 mg, black rock
salt 9.2 mg, Ferula narthex (asafoetida) 1.9 mg, Citrus aurantifolia 0.1 mg,
excipients 40.9 mg.

Action:
  • Pacifies Vata: ↓ Pitta: ↓↓ Kapha: ↓
  • Balances Bodhaka Kapha (appetite and sense of taste), Udana Vata (movement through esophagus to stomach), Samana Vata (regulation and motility of gastrointestinal tract), Pachaka Pitta (secretory element of digestive glands) and Kledaka Kapha (function of mucous membrans)
  • Results in pure Rasa free of Ama and in healthy Dhatus
  • Nourishes Rasa - Rakta

Contraindications: None

Precautions: None

Dosage:
1-2 tablets after meals with warm water or salty lassi (= dilute 1 part of fresh
yoghurt with 3 parts of water and mix well).

Packaging: 60 tablets of 0.5 g

Shelf life: 3 years
